Understanding our communities

Three types of spaces, each designed for a different kind of connection.

Circle

A small group, built on trust

Circles are 8–12 women who meet monthly, peer-led, and follow free Lean In curriculum. They're the heart of the platform — intimate, accountable, and free to join.

Find one ↑
Network

Your city or industry, organized

Networks are larger communities organized around where you live or what you do — hundreds to thousands of members, focused on events and inspiration. Many Circles form through Networks.

Find one ↑
Group

A topic, open to everyone

Groups are online discussion spaces organized around a topic — post and connect anytime, open to all members. Great for questions, sharing wins, and finding people who get it.
