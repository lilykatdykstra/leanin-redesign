Sarah Chen
Senior PM at Stripe · 2h ago · SF Women in Tech Circle

Just had my annual review and successfully negotiated a 22% raise — something I genuinely wasn't sure I could do six months ago. The thing that shifted everything? Our Circle's session on anchoring and BATNA.

I walked in with a specific number backed by market research, stayed calm when they pushed back, and actually said "I need a moment to think about that" out loud. The silence didn't kill me. They came back with an offer 18% above where we started.

If you're preparing to negotiate: know your number, know theirs, and practice saying it out loud until it feels normal. You've got this.

VP Engineering at Salesforce · 5h ago · Lean In Bay Area

Recap from last night's Women in Tech Leadership Summit — truly one of the most energizing evenings I've had in years. A room full of 200+ women who are building, leading, and changing the game.

Three things I'm taking away:

1/ Sponsorship > mentorship. Mentors give advice; sponsors give opportunities. Find sponsors, be sponsors.
2/ "No" is a complete sentence in your personal life. Protect your energy like the finite resource it is.
3/ The loneliness of leadership is real — community like this one is the antidote.

Slides and recording coming to the Network page this week. Who else was there?
