Lean In Circles

Your closest
accountability group

A Lean In Circle is a small peer group — typically 6 to 12 women — who meet regularly to support each other's professional and personal growth. Think of it as your board of advisors, your safe space to practice speaking up, and your most consistent source of real feedback.

6–12 members
2× per month
90% feel more confident
  • Structured meeting agendas focused on goal-setting and accountability
  • Private, members-only discussion space
  • Peer-led — no formal facilitator required
  • Matched by career stage, industry, or location
